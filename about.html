An in-depth preview of the future

The Nintendo AR Trails is a new console that breaks the plane between the outdoor and gaming experiences. Of course, Nintendo is no stranger to infusing augmented reality into our games, but the headset features new, ground-breaking technology to perfect the experience.


  1. A system of cameras reads the surrounding landscape.
  2. The headset hardware reads it.
  3. Then, it projects the immersive elements onto your shades.

You can wear it with or without its handles, thanks to the built-in strap for extra stability. Additionally, removing them allows you to store it on the console, which charges the headset. The console itself can hold up to 6 headsets at once, making it the perfect tool for group trips.




Whether you're at home, the local park, or hiking through the woods,
 whether you're solo, with a partner, or a group of friends,
 AR Trails is geared to give you the ultimate immersive experience within your environment.
